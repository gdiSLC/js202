Food Delivery

Two scenarios:

  1. You're cramming for a huge test and you're hungry. You stop studying, go to the kitchen, pull out your food, and cook it. You take your food back to your study table to eat. End result: you waste 20-30 minutes of your study time making food.
  2. You're cramming for a huge test and you're hungry. You call your favorite restaurant and order delivery. You continue studying and eat your food out of the carton when it arrives. End result: you spend $20, and you can keep studying.


Scenario 2: asynchronous.
